# Privacy Policy — Phantom-WG iOS

**Effective date: April 9, 2026**

Phantom-WG iOS ("the App") is a WireGuard VPN client developed by Rıza Emre ARAS. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Summary

**The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** There are no accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting, and no third-party SDKs.

## Data Handling

### What the App Stores

- **Tunnel configurations** (WireGuard private keys, server addresses, DNS settings) are stored exclusively in the iOS Keychain on your device. These are never transmitted to Phantom-WG infrastructure or any third party.
- **Language preference** (English or Turkish) is stored in local UserDefaults on your device.

### What the App Does NOT Collect

- No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- No device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, serial number, etc.)
- No usage analytics or behavioral data
- No crash reports or diagnostic data
- No location data
- No browsing history or DNS queries
- No network traffic logs

### Network Connections

The App connects **only** to the VPN server addresses specified in your imported configuration files. These are servers you own and operate. The App does not contact any Phantom-WG servers, update servers, analytics endpoints, or any other remote service.

In Ghost Mode, the App additionally connects to a wstunnel server address specified in your configuration. This is also a server you own and operate.

### Third-Party Code

The App includes the following open-source components:

- **WireGuardKit** — WireGuard protocol implementation ([wireguard.com](https://www.wireguard.com))
- **wstunnel** — WebSocket tunnel for Ghost Mode ([github.com/ARAS-Workspace/wstunnel](https://github.com/ARAS-Workspace/wstunnel))

Neither of these components collect or transmit any data independently.

### Privacy Manifest

The App includes an Apple Privacy Manifest (`PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy`) that declares no tracked data types and no required reason APIs beyond those needed for VPN functionality.
